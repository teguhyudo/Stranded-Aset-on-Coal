## 5. Additional Observations

**Open Access:** Elsevier's hybrid OA options apply to most journals above. Energy Policy, Ecological Economics, and Journal of Cleaner Production have higher OA uptake. If ViriyaENB funding covers APCs, Energy Policy (APC ~€3,450) or Energy Economics (APC ~€3,900) would maximise access for Indonesian policymakers.

**Preprint strategy:** Posting to SSRN or the Munich Personal RePEC Archive (MPRA) before submission is standard in environmental economics and finance. This builds visibility and enables feedback; most journals on this list allow preprints at submission. Recommended: post to SSRN simultaneously with submitting to Energy Economics.

**Supplementary materials:** All journals accept online appendices for robustness tables and extended methodology. The bipartite network figure, vulnerability heatmap, and tornado diagram should appear in the main text; more detailed bank-level tables can move to the appendix to stay within page limits.

**Conference circuit before submission:** Consider presenting at IAEE (International Association for Energy Economics), European Finance Association (EFA), or the NGFS/BIS climate risk workshops. Conference exposure can generate pre-submission referee contacts and significantly reduces the risk of desk rejection due to "unknown authors."
